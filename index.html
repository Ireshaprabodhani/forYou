My Truth
There are so many things I keep locked inside because I am terrified I don’t know how to say them correctly.
Sometimes, when you don't respond for hours, it feels like the air leaves my lungs. My heart feels heavy, and it hurts a little. I'm sorry if that sounds silly... but I wait for you more than I want to admit.
I often want to pick up the phone and call you just to hear your voice, but I stop myself every single time. I am so afraid that you will get bored of me and leave me behind.
I will never forget the first day you messaged me and called me "Dhoni." You have no idea how much I love that name... my heart stops for a second every time you’re the one saying it.
I don't know what place I have in your mind or your heart. It scares me not knowing. But whatever you feel, I hope you’ll share it with me soon. Please know that I could never be angry with you.
I just want you to be safe. I want you to be careful. Above all, I want you to be happy—even if that happiness isn't with me. I am eagerly waiting for the day I finally see your name calling my phone.
I’m always here for you. No matter what. ❤️
